Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 6, 2026

Connor Casey built the cwote app as a Commercial app. This SERVICE is provided by Connor Casey and is intended for use as is.

1. Information Collection and Use

cwote is designed with a "privacy-first" philosophy. cwote stores all data locally. There is no data collection, no tracking, and no third-party SDKs used for data harvesting.

  • Local Storage: All text, notes, and quotes you create are stored exclusively on your device.
  • No Personal Data: We do not collect or store any personal information, such as your name or email, on our servers at this time.
  • No Third-Party SDKs: We do not use any third-party marketing, analytics, or tracking SDKs.

2. Artificial Intelligence Integration

The app offers an optional feature allowing users to interact with Claude (an AI model by Anthropic) to discuss or refine their stories. Please be aware that when you use this feature, the specific text you send to the AI is processed by Anthropic's cloud services. This content is not stored or accessed by the developer of cwote.

3. iCloud and Backups

If you have enabled iCloud backups on your iOS device, your app data may be backed up to Apple's servers. This process is governed entirely by Apple's Privacy Policy and your personal device settings.

4. Future Updates

As cwote evolves, we may introduce features such as user accounts, cloud synchronization, and social sharing. If and when these features are added, this Privacy Policy will be updated to reflect how that data is handled and stored in our secure backend.
